Buttons

Default Button - You should us the following formatting:

<span class="button">Button Text</span>
or
<a class="button" href="...">Button Text</a>
or
<input type="submit" class="button" value="Button Text"/>

Small White Button - You should us the following formatting:

<span class="w-button">Button Text</span>
or
<a class="w-button" href="...">Button Text</a>
or
<input type="submit" class="w-button" value="Button Text"/>

Big Black Button - You should us the following formatting:

<span class="bb-button">Button Text</span>
or
